**Effective date:** February 20, 2026

Kart Track Park ("we", "the service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## What We Collect

- **Account info:** Your email address, used to identify your account.
- **Logged data:** Data you create through the service.

## What We Don't Collect

- We do not use analytics or tracking scripts.
- We do not use cookies (authentication is token-based).
- We do not collect device fingerprints or IP addresses for tracking purposes.

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Kart Track Park service to you.

## Third Parties

- **AWS:** Our infrastructure runs on Amazon Web Services (Lambda, S3, CloudFront).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion by contacting us.

## Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AWS encryption).

## Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance.

Questions? Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/BrianLeishman/karttrackpark.com).
